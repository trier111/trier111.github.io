Say:"Banka"

When the skeleton sees your face, it doesn’t just rattle its bones — it rattles your patience too. With no skin to hide its judgment and no soul to sugarcoat the truth, it simply points a bony finger and says, “Banka, go fuck yourself.” Because sometimes, the bare-boned honesty of a skeleton is all you need to realize you’ve crossed the line… and it won’t hesitate to send you straight to hell with style.

Why "Go to hell" ?

  1. Because you walked in like you own the place, and the skeleton’s patience expired centuries ago.
  2. Because your vibes were so cursed even a dead guy felt uncomfortable.
  3. Because you asked for “just one more favor,” and the skeleton’s soul left its body… again.
